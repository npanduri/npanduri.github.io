Experience the SUSAN aircraft

SUSAN, which stands for SUbsonic Single Aft eNgine (SUSAN) Electrofan, is an aircraft concept designed to reduce environmental pollution caused by current aircrafts. The aircraft accomplishes this goal by having a several important components: a turbofan for traveling long distances, multiple electric engines for efficiency and reduced pollution during short distance travel, t-tail for increased aerodynamics, and natural laminar flow techniques are implemented in the wingbox to reduce drag.
